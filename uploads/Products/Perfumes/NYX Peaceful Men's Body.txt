NYX Peaceful Men's Body Splash

A charming, vibrant and refreshing body splash, with a fruity, vanilla, musk and floral scent. It is also rich in essential oils and natural emollients that moisturize and rejuvenate the skin, in addition to perfuming and refreshing the body throughout the day. Splash is a lighter alternative to perfumes, making it ideal for daily use or for those who prefer a more delicate scent. What distinguishes it is its 100% raw materials and that it is licensed by the Ministry of Health
