Construction Skills Book for Children Dar Al Qamar Educational Resources

Age Group: 4 - 7 Years

About the Book:

The book deals with basic mathematical concepts:

(Short - Long - Shortest - Tallest - Big - Small - Biggest - Smallest ..)

In a modern style, relying on logical thinking skills as a basic approach in formulating the exercises accompanying each concept

(Selecting the pattern - Matching - Difference and similarity - Puzzles - Completing the pattern)

Taking geometric shapes and educational tools close to the child's environment as a basic means to clarify the concepts in the book
