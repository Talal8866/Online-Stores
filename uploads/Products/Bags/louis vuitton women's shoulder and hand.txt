louis vuitton women's shoulder and hand bag

A sports shoulder bag made of thick, padded, water-resistant fabric, with a large layer and a suitable size to accommodate many necessary sports items. It contains two pockets inside to put small items that can be lost and a small pocket to put money or a personal card. The second layer is in the front and has a smaller size to separate items from each other. It is also equipped with a back pocket with a zipper for easy access to small items quickly, such as keys or money. It is also equipped with an internal USB connection.
